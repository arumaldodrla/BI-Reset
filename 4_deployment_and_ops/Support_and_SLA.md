# Support & Service Level Agreement (SLA)

**Project**: BI Reset
**Version**: 1.0
**Date**: December 12, 2025

## 1. Introduction

This document defines the support structure and service level commitments for the BI Reset platform.

## 2. Support Channels

- **Email Support**: For non-urgent issues, users can contact the support team at `support@bireset.com`.
- **In-App Chat**: For real-time assistance, a chat widget will be available within the BI Reset application.
- **Knowledge Base**: A self-service portal with articles, tutorials, and FAQs will be available at `help.bireset.com`.

## 3. Support Hours

- Standard support will be available from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM (UTC), Monday through Friday.
- Emergency support for critical issues will be available 24/7.

## 4. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

### 4.1. Uptime Guarantee

- The BI Reset platform will have a guaranteed uptime of **99.9%** per month.
- Uptime is calculated as: `(Total Minutes in Month - Downtime Minutes) / Total Minutes in Month * 100`.
- Downtime does not include scheduled maintenance.

### 4.2. Response Times

| Priority | Description | Response Time |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Critical** | Platform is down or a core feature is unusable for all users. | **1 Hour** (24/7) |
| **High** | A core feature is significantly degraded or unusable for a subset of users. | **4 Business Hours** |
| **Medium** | A non-critical feature is not working as expected. | **8 Business Hours** |
| **Low** | Minor cosmetic issues or general questions. | **24 Business Hours** |

### 4.3. Scheduled Maintenance

- Scheduled maintenance will be performed outside of peak business hours.
- Users will be notified at least 48 hours in advance of any scheduled maintenance that is expected to cause downtime.

## 5. Exclusions

This SLA does not apply to any performance or availability issues that are:

- Caused by factors outside of our reasonable control (e.g., force majeure events, internet access problems).
- A result of user error or unauthorized actions.
- Related to the performance of third-party services, including data sources (Zoho, SAP) or the LLM provider (OpenAI).
